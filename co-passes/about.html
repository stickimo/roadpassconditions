About RoadPassConditions

Why this exists

Dispatchers, guides, and family road-trip planners need mountain pass info in one glance. RoadPassConditions.com keeps it intentionally simple: static pages, real weather, human-readable summaries. The app translates NWS forecasts into travel, traction, and closure guidance with wind/ice-aware notes for each pass.

Data sources

  • Weather: National Weather Service (api.weather.gov grid forecasts).
